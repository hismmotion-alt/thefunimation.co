Featured Case Study · Public Awareness

ISMED-CLIM — Climate & Public Health Awareness

Funimation partnered with the EU-funded ISMED-CLIM initiative to produce a series of engaging 2D explainer animations that communicate the health impacts of climate change across the Mediterranean. Designed for a broad public audience, the videos simplify complex scientific and medical topics into clear, accessible stories, helping raise awareness about heat exposure, air pollution, vulnerable populations, and innovative climate-health solutions. Through friendly character animation and modern motion graphics, we transformed research-driven content into engaging educational experiences that inform and inspire action.

Client ISMED-CLIM · EU-funded initiative
Deliverables 2D explainer animation, character animation, motion graphics
Focus Climate change, public health, education
